About Us

PageTurner Bookshop has been a beloved part of the community since 2024. Founded by lifelong readers Tawana, Sam and MK, our shop offers a carefully hand-picked selection of new, classic, and rare titles across all genres. We believe that every person deserves to find a book that changes their life — and we are here to help you find yours. Whether you are searching for a gripping thriller, an inspiring biography, or a colourful children's story, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to guide you.
