About Us

PageTurner Bookshop has been a beloved part of the community since 2024. Founded by lifelong readers Tawana, Sam and MK our shop offers a carefully hand-picked selection of new, classic, and rare titles across all genres. We believe that every person deserves to find a book that changes their life — and we are here to help you find yours.

  • Over 5,000 titles available in store at any time
  • Friendly and knowledgeable staff to help you choose
  • Weekly book club meetings every Thursday evening
  • Gift wrapping service available on request
  • Special orders placed within 48 hours
